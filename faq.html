FAQ

Answers to the most common questions about MOONVIC.

Is MOONVIC a meme token?

MOONVIC has meme energy in its branding, but the intent is to build a real ecosystem with tools, utilities, and a long-term brand.

Which network is MOONVIC on?

MOONVIC is deployed on the Ethereum network as an ERC-20 token.

Is there a tax on buys or sells?

If you decide to implement taxes, you can describe them here. If not, you can state that MOONVIC has 0% buy/sell tax.

Where can I see updates?

Official updates will be shared through the website and your chosen social channels (X/Twitter, Telegram, Discord, etc.).
