How to Buy MOONVIC

A simple step-by-step guide to joining the MOONVIC movement.

Step 1 — Install a Crypto Wallet

Download a trusted Ethereum wallet such as MetaMask, Coinbase Wallet, or Trust Wallet.

Step 2 — Add Ethereum (ETH)

Purchase ETH through your wallet or a centralized exchange like Coinbase, Binance, or Kraken. Transfer ETH to your wallet if needed.

Step 3 — Visit Uniswap

Go to app.uniswap.org and connect your wallet.

Step 4 — Enter the MOONVIC Contract Address

Paste the official MOONVIC contract address: 0x3c7462004dd99b680b3736982a965effc7d285aa

Step 5 — Swap ETH for MOONVIC

Choose the amount of ETH you want to swap, review the details, and confirm the transaction.

Step 6 — You're In

Once the transaction confirms, you officially hold MOONVIC — welcome to the movement.
