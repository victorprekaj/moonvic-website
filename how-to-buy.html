How to Buy MOONVIC

A simple, cinematic, step‑by‑step guide for new holders entering the MOONVIC universe.

Step 1 — Create a Wallet

To hold MOONVIC (MVIC), you need a Web3 wallet. The most popular options are:

  • MetaMask
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Download your preferred wallet and follow the setup instructions.

Step 2 — Buy Ethereum (ETH)

MOONVIC runs on the Ethereum network. You’ll need ETH to swap for MVIC and to pay gas fees.

You can buy ETH on exchanges like:

  • Coinbase
  • Binance
  • Kraken
  • Crypto.com

Step 3 — Connect to a Decentralized Exchange (DEX)

Once you have ETH in your wallet, connect to a DEX such as:

  • Uniswap
  • SushiSwap

Make sure your wallet is connected to the Ethereum network.

Step 4 — Swap ETH for MVIC

Enter the official MOONVIC contract address:

Contract Address:
0x3c7462004dd99b680b3736982a965effc7d285aa

Select MVIC, choose the amount you want to buy, and confirm the transaction. After a few seconds, MOONVIC will appear in your wallet.
