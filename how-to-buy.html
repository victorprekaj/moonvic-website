How to Buy

Your step‑by‑step guide to joining the MOONVIC movement.

Step 1 — Install a Crypto Wallet

Download a trusted wallet such as MetaMask or Trust Wallet. These allow you to store, send, and receive MVIC tokens.

Step 2 — Add Ethereum to Your Wallet

Purchase ETH through your wallet or a centralized exchange. You’ll need ETH to swap for MOONVIC and to cover gas fees.

Step 3 — Connect to Uniswap

Visit Uniswap and connect your wallet. Make sure you’re on the Ethereum network.
