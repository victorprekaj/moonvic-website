How to Buy MOONVIC

Step-by-step for new and experienced users.

Step 1 — Get a Wallet

Install a Web3 wallet like MetaMask, Trust Wallet, or another Ethereum-compatible wallet. Make sure you safely back up your seed phrase.

Step 2 — Fund with ETH

Buy ETH on a centralized exchange or within your wallet, then send it to your Web3 wallet address on the Ethereum network.

Step 3 — Connect to a DEX

Open a decentralized exchange (DEX) like Uniswap, connect your wallet, and ensure you are on the Ethereum network.

Step 4 — Swap ETH for MOONVIC (MV)

Paste the official MOONVIC contract address, select MV, and swap your desired amount of ETH. Always double-check the contract address before confirming.

Step 5 — Add Token to Wallet

Add the MOONVIC contract address to your wallet’s custom tokens so your MV balance appears correctly.
