How to Buy MOONVIC

A simple, cinematic guide for new holders entering the MOONVIC universe.

Step 1 — Create a Wallet

To hold MOONVIC (MVIC), you need a Web3 wallet. The most popular options are:

  • MetaMask
  • Trust Wallet
  • Coinbase Wallet

Download your preferred wallet and follow the setup instructions.

Step 2 — Buy Ethereum (ETH)

MOONVIC runs on the Ethereum network. You’ll need ETH to swap for MVIC and to pay gas fees.
