MOONVIC Roadmap

A cinematic journey of ambition, innovation, and long‑term vision.

Phase 1 — Foundation

The beginning of the MOONVIC universe. A phase focused on stability, identity, and establishing the core pillars of the project.

  • Token creation and Ethereum mainnet deployment
  • Brand identity development (black‑and‑gold cinematic aesthetic)
  • Website launch with premium multi‑page structure
  • Initial community formation
  • Smart contract verification

Phase 2 — Expansion

With the foundation set, MOONVIC expands outward — building momentum, visibility, and trust.

  • Listings on major tracking platforms (CoinGecko, CoinMarketCap)
  • Marketing campaigns and community growth initiatives
  • Partnership outreach and ecosystem alignment
  • Liquidity strengthening and market stability

Phase 3 — Utility & Ecosystem

MOONVIC evolves from a token into a functional ecosystem — unlocking real value and long‑term purpose.

  • Utility integrations and platform partnerships
  • Staking or rewards‑based mechanisms (if aligned with long‑term vision)
  • MOONVIC ecosystem tools and community utilities
  • Brand collaborations and Web3 activations

Phase 4 — The MOONVIC Universe

The long‑term vision — a fully realized, cinematic, and interconnected MOONVIC ecosystem.

  • Major ecosystem expansion
  • Cross‑platform integrations
  • Advanced utilities and long‑term sustainability features
  • Global community events and brand activations

The roadmap is not a limit — it’s a launchpad. MOONVIC is built to grow, evolve, and expand far beyond expectations.
